About The Event

The VIRTUSA Organization brings you "HACKATHON", a 3 day Technical Festival that involves a 24hrs Hackathon, Coding Combat, Treasure Hunt Game and So many brain storming & Fun Battles with a vision to create a perfect innovative atmosphere for students in Greater Noida to learn, share, grow, innovate and network with like minded people. The most awaited Event of Greater Noida and G L Bajaj where you will get everything for a memorable weekend.

About the Hackathon

The event will be conducted just like traditional hackathons, a 24hr sprint of programming. Your goal should be to start from scratch and end with a working prototype modelling your solution. Themes of the hackathon will be released soon. The solution could be anything from a mobile app, a website to a desktop software. The teams will have complete freedom on how they want to approach the idea, the only condition being that it should solve a problem or bring innovation in the field of social applications of technology. Advocating an ideology to recognize and reward innovation, This Hackathon offers exciting prizes to the winners. Participants will be judged in regular assessments throughout the 24 hours of the hackathon. Virtusa includes all food and accommodation facilities for the participants. Register for a fun filled experience brainstorming with like minded people, with the bonus of an overnight coding spree.
